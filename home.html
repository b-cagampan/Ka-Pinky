About Organization

Ka-Pinky is a service organization for Leni Robredo, the running president. We want to inform people about Leni's platforms, educational background, senate slate, and everything else they need to know before voting for her. The majority of us have either made or heard of a pinky pledge. A pinky promise, for the uninformed, is the act of tying your pinky fingers together to represent a promise. Leni Robredo promises the Filipinos concrete solutions as well as long- and short-term goals.
